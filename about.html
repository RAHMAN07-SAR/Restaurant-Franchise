About Xabite

Serving happiness on a plate since 2010, with the best burgers, sandwiches, and smiles in Bengaluru.

Our Story

Founded in the heart of Bengaluru, Xabite has grown from a small burger joint into a beloved franchise across the city. We focus on fresh ingredients, bold flavors, and a welcoming experience for all our guests.

Our chefs craft every dish with passion and creativity, ensuring that every bite is a moment to remember. Whether it’s a quick snack or a family gathering, we bring joy to your table.
